Checklist

#1 Good Sleep

You should get a good rest the night before, sleeping is incredibly important for your health and will charge you of energy for your next adventure.

#2 Get a bag

Yes, sounds obvious but it's one of the most fundamental things you will need for your hiking trip as will help you to transport everything you need.

#3 Water & Snacks

Water is fundamental and together with some snacks you will be able to recover energy and strength use during long walks and intensity activity.

#4 Compass & Map

You will need a compass and map to navigate on long and unmarked trails. Don't relay only on latest technology as GPS and phones, you might finish without battery.

#5 First Aid Kit

First Aid Kit is essential, you never know when you will need it but it will be good to have it when it happend.

#6 Hiking Shoes

Last but not least important, a good pair of hiking shoes will give you protection and comfort to face any long or short trail.

You are ready to go
